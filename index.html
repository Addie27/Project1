Motivation

The Philadelphia public school district is the eighth largest in the nation and severely lacking in financial resources to offer the high quality schooling children deserve and need to fuel their interests in fields like technology. This, paired with the social pressures and academic stress of school makes self-directed learning even more appealing to school-age children. There are a variety of tech-related learning programs and events that take a fun, hands-on approach to teach coding or building hardware while providing a supportive community. However, it may be difficult for students to take the initiative to locate the proper resources to fuel their interests.
